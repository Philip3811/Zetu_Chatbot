***AI-Powered Customer Support Chatbot***
**Overview**

This project implements an AI-supported customer knowledge chatbot designed to automate customer support interactions for a commerce platform. The chatbot talks with users through WhatsApp, not just sending messages but actually trying to “understand” what people mean, even when they type in strange ways or half sentences. It can pull out real-time data about orders, delivery progress, discounts, product details, and even account information, all straight from the company’s database though sometimes it may seem like it’s thinking too much before replying. The system behaves almost like a digital clerk that never sleeps, though it occasionally repeats itself when the same question is asked twice, which is oddly human. It’s meant to make the conversation feel natural, or at least close enough that customers forget they’re chatting with code.

The system was built to improve operational efficiency, response time, and customer satisfaction by reducing reliance on human agents for repetitive support tasks. It’s supposed to make things faster but also somehow more personal, which sounds contradictory but works in practice. The idea is that by letting the AI handle the boring stuff, the human team can focus on the complicated or emotional cases, though sometimes the AI over-explains simple things. In a sense, it’s not just automation—it’s a kind of reorganization of attention, where machines take over the noise and people handle the nuance. The efficiency part is measurable, but the satisfaction part, well, that depends on who you ask and what day it is.

**Key Features**
- Natural language customer interaction via WhatsApp
- AI-driven understanding of user queries
- Secure, real-time querying of production databases
- Context-aware responses based on customer session history

- Automatic handling of common support workflows:
       - Order status & delivery tracking
       - Product and pricing inquiries
       - Discounts and promotions
       - Account-related questions
